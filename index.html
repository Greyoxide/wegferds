Our focus is the customer.

Wegferds' Printing and Publications is a well established business founded in North Bend, Oregon. We are located on the beautiful Oregon coastline and easy to find on the 101 highway. Due to our convenient location, we are easily accessible to all of our customers. We base high value on being a local, family-owned and operated company that has been in business for the last 50yrs. We know our community and have great pride in being a valued member of it. We prioritize meeting our customers' needs as well as developing a personal relationship with them. Basically, we know if we take the time to listen, we'll be able to address their printing needs the best.

We have the equipment and experience to design and create almost any job imaginable. Not only that, but we're willing to stay up all night if that's what it needs. We design and print documents, signs, banners, advertising ideas, business cards, logos, invitations, announcements, calenders, books, manuscripts, magazines, brochures, pamphlets, posters, fliers, pens, mugs, clothing, presentations, stickers, labels, resumes, and much, much more. Just ask us, and see what we can do for you!
